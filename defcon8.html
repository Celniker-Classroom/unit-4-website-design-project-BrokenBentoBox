Harry Potter: The Cursed Child

Summary:

"Harry Potter and the Cursed Child" is a stage play set nineteen years after
the original series. The story focuses on Harry Potter’s son, Albus Severus
Potter, who feels pressure from his father’s legacy and becomes friends with
Scorpius Malfoy, Draco’s son. Together, they use a time-turner to travel into
the past and try to change history, but their actions create serious problems
in the present. The play is told in two parts and is performed live in
theaters, making it different from the original novels, which were written as
books instead of scripts for the stage.
