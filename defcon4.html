DEFCON Level #4

Descrption:

The second lowest level of DEFCON is DEFCON 4. It means all is normal, but there is increased military alertness. During this level, there can be some possible threats, not nothing important yet. Security measures are tightened a little more than normal. Troops continue their regular routine while staying alert.
