Harry Potter: The Goblet of Fire

Summary:

In "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire," Harry returns to Hogwarts for his
fourth year, where the school is hosting the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous
competition between three wizarding schools. Although underage, Harry is
mysteriously chosen as a fourth champion. He faces three difficult tasks:
retrieving a golden egg from a dragon, rescuing friends from the depths of the
lake, and navigating a dangerous maze. With the help of his friends and quick
thinking, Harry completes each challenge. During the final task, Harry and
Cedric Diggory are transported by a portkey to a graveyard, where Lord
Voldemort returns to full power. Cedric is killed, and Harry barely escapes
with Voldemort chasing him. When Harry returns to Hogwarts, he tells all
that Voldemort is back, but the Ministry of Magic refuses to believe him.
