Harry Potter: The Half-Blood Prince

Summary:

In "Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince," Harry starts his sixth year at
Hogwarts while Voldemort’s influence grows stronger. Dumbledore gives Harry
private lessons, sharing memories that reveal how Voldemort sought immortality
by creating Horcruxes. A Horcrux is a dark magical object that holds a fragment
of a wizard’s soul, making the person very difficult to destroy. Voldemort hid
parts of his soul in several Horcruxes, so he could survive even if his body
was destroyed. Harry excels in Potions class with the help of a mysterious old
textbook once owned by the Half-Blood Prince. He also notices Draco Malfoy
acting suspiciously and forms a closer relationship with Ginny Weasley.
Dumbledore and Harry travel to a hidden cave to find one of Voldemort’s
Horcruxes, but what they retrieve is actually a fake. When they return to
Hogwarts, Death Eaters attack the school, and Snape kills Dumbledore. Harry
decides leave school and find/destroy the real Horcruxes.
