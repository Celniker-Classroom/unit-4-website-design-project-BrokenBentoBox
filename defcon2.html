Harry Potter: The Chamber of Secrets

Summary:

In "Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets," Harry returns to Hogwarts for
his second year, despite warnings from Dobby, a mysterious house-elf.
Strange things start happening at the school, students are getting very scared,
and a message on the wall warns that the Chamber of Secrets has been opened. Harry,
Ron, and Hermione investigate and discover that the chamber is connected to a
legend about a monster hidden within Hogwarts. As the attacks continue,
suspicion falls on Harry, but he eventually uncovers that Tom Riddle, a memory
preserved in a diary, is behind the events. With help from Fawkes the Phoenix
and the Sorting Hat, Harry defeats the basilisk, saves Ginny Weasley, and
destroys the diary, ending the curse.
