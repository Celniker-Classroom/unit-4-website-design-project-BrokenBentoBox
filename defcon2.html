DEFCON Level #2

Descrption:

The second highest level of DEFCON is DEFCON 2. It means we are very close to a war. During this level, the U.S. military is ready to deploy at any moment. The U.S. reached DEFCON 2 during the Cuban Missile Crisis in the 1960s. Also activated in 1991, during the Gulf War.
