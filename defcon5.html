Harry Potter: The Order of the Phoenix

Summary:

In "Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix," Harry returns to Hogwarts for
his fifth year, frustrated that most witches and wizards refuse to believe
Voldemort has come back. The Ministry of Magic tries to control Hogwarts by
appointing Dolores Umbridge as the new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher.
Umbridge’s harsh rules and refusal to teach practical magic lead Harry,
Hermione, and Ron to form Dumbledore’s Army, secretly training students in
defense spells. Throughout the year, Harry has strange dreams connecting him
to Voldemort’s mind. When he sees a vision of Sirius Black in danger at the
Ministry, Harry leads his friends into a trap set by Death Eaters. A battle
follows, and Sirius is killed. The Ministry of Magic finally confirms
Voldemort and his army of Death Eaters have returned to the wizarding world.
