DEFCON Level #1

Descrption:

The highest level of DEFCON is DEFCON 1. It means that a war(large scale), is happening or about to. During this level the U.S. military is 100% prepared to respond immediately to threats. DEFCON would mean our country is in an extreme crisis. So far the U.S. has never officially made a DEFCON 1 alert in history.
