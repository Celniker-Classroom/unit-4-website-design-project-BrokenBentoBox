Harry Potter: The Deathly Hallows

Summary:

In "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows," Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave
Hogwarts to find and destroy the Horcruxes that keep Voldemort alive. The
wizard world is very unsafe, with Death Eaters taking control of the Ministry
of Magic and Hogwarts. Harry and his friends are forced to hide and move from
place to place while looking for clues. They break into the Ministry and
Gringotts Bank to get Horcruxes and learn about the Deathly Hallows, three
powerful magic objects that Voldemort also wants. The story is split into two
movies. The first part shows Harry, Ron, and Hermione on the run, searching for
Horcruxes and facing many dangers. In the second part, there is a huge battle
at Hogwarts as everyone fights against Voldemort and his followers. Many
important characters, like Fred Weasley, Remus Lupin, Tonks, and Snape, are
killed. Harry gives himself up to Voldemort but survives, and in the end, he
destroys the last Horcrux and defeats Voldemort. Yay!
