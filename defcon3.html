Harry Potter: The Prisoner of Azkaban

Summary:

Harry Potter, an orphan living with cruel relatives, learns he’s a wizard and starts at Hogwarts. He’s famous for surviving Voldemort’s attack as a baby. With Ron and Hermione, Harry discovers the Philosopher’s Stone is hidden at school. They suspect Snape, but it’s timid Professor Quirrell hosting a weak Voldemort on the back of his head. After passing magical trials, Harry confronts Quirrell; the Stone appears in Harry’s pocket because he wants it but not to use it. Quirrell disintegrates when he touches Harry. Dumbledore explains Voldemort killed Harry’s parents, and the Stone is destroyed for safety.
