DEFCON Levels:

Descrption

DEFCON stands for Defense Readiness Condition. It is alert system of 5 levels, used by U.S military to signal threats against our country. The system was developed by the United States Join Chiefs of Staff, (or JCS) in November of 1959. It is a progressive readiness system for all parts of the U.S military. It overtook past systems known like SAC (Strategic Air Command, 1946–1992) and Color-Coded War Plans (1920–1939).
